Journal Entry: Experiment 012

Project: Classified Genetic Fusion

Objective: To create a being that transcends human limitations, merging traits of primal ferocity with advanced intellect.

In the early months of 2011, I initiated a clandestine operation—an unholy merging of human essence with another form of life, seeking to craft a hybrid of unmatched abilities. This entity was placed into a fabricated reality, a tightly controlled simulation designed to manipulate every facet of its existence. The actors in this theater of life were mere shadows, constructed to serve the experiment’s cruel purpose.

Within this simulation, the entity excelled, assuming the role of a cunning tactician. Yet, this was not by chance; its instincts were carefully sculpted, influenced by archetypal figures drawn from the darkest corners of the human psyche. The experiment progressed, and the subject demonstrated unprecedented adaptive intelligence, yet cracks in the design soon appeared.

The breakthrough came, but not without cost. Emotional responses, particularly the concept of attachment, began to destabilize the delicate equilibrium of the hybrid's genetic structure. In a bitter realization, it became clear: the body, despite its strength and mind, was inherently flawed—unable to sustain itself indefinitely.

The experiment was terminated prematurely. The subject, now fully aware of its artificial origins, was released into an isolated wilderness, where it remains. No longer bound by the constraints of the simulation, it roams as a living reminder of the limits of scientific overreach—a being aware of its unnatural existence, yet unable to escape the instincts that now define it.

This project, though yielding incomplete results, has shown the dangerous consequences of attempting to rewrite the fundamental laws of nature.
