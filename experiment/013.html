Experiment 013 - The 2012 Apocalypse

Date: December 21st, 2012

The world stood on the edge of chaos, fearing an impending apocalypse foretold by ancient calendars. What they didn’t know, however, was that the event was no cosmic coincidence. It was orchestrated by a group of rogue scientists— led by a deranged visionary obsessed with the collapse of civilization.

The experiment, labeled simply as **013**, began years earlier. The goal was clear: to create global panic, a mental plague so intense it would tear the fabric of reality itself. Utilizing advanced neurotechnology, the team implanted subliminal fears into the minds of millions through digital media, triggering nightmares, paranoia, and uncontrollable dread. The world saw it as a "mystical prophecy," unaware that the real culprits were human.

As December 21st approached, the world began to spiral into madness. Entire cities fell into chaos. People mutilated themselves, unable to bear the weight of the apocalypse they were convinced was coming. The human mind began to fracture. The streets were filled with blood, the air thick with despair. The end seemed inevitable.

The Final Stage

On the day of the supposed apocalypse, the final phase was initiated. The scientists, twisted in their delusion, deployed a weaponized form of "mental collapse." This was no longer just a mind game—it was a full assault on the human psyche. People who had already been teetering on the edge were driven to madness. Mass suicides, riots, and uncontrollable violence spread like wildfire.

The true horror was the collective hopelessness. Parents watched their children descend into insanity, while loved ones turned on each other in blind fear. The world was burning—not by fire, but by the torment of the mind.

The Intervention

In the heart of the chaos, something changed. Just as the world seemed on the brink of permanent destruction, a blinding light erupted in the sky—something far beyond human understanding. The source of the light was unknown, but its impact was immediate. The madness that had consumed billions began to subside. The screams quieted, and the violence halted.

It was as if a higher power had intervened, pulling humanity back from the edge of its own undoing. The light wasn’t a mere celestial event—it was salvation. The mental fog lifted, and for the first time in months, there was clarity. The apocalypse was halted, but the damage had been done.

The world was saved, but scars remained. The experiment had nearly destroyed the human race, and the perpetrators? They vanished without a trace. No records, no evidence—just a world left to heal from wounds far deeper than flesh.
